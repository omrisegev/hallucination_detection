Why this page exists. The competitor numbers lived in two files with incompatible provenance. published_baselines.csv (6 cells) carries a source column naming paper and table. scores_lsml_upcr.csv (the other 13 cells) carries no citation at all — no paper, no table, no page, no scale note. A claim that we are competitive with published work is only as good as those numbers, so each was checked against papers/extracted/ — the raw extracted text, not the digest cards.

62 of 62 rows verified against the paper's own table, covering 7 papers: EPR, HCPD, ARS, Noise Injection, Semantic Energy, ALS/FEPoID and HARP. The rest could not be checked because the paper is not in papers/.

Two corrections this audit produced, both applied to the grid:
  • HARP is supervised, not unsupervised. The paper trains its detector with a binary cross-entropy loss on hallucination labels. It had been recorded as an unsupervised competitor, which would have made an unfair comparison look fair.
  • The stored HARP anchor was the wrong model row. 92.8 is the Qwen-2.5-7B-Instruct result; our cell uses Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, for which the paper reports 92.9. The stored value was cross-model.
What could not be verified. No local copy exists for LapEigvals (5 cells — and it is also the G3 decision gate in the roadmap), INSIDE (2 cells), LOS-Net (1 cell plus its 11 baseline rows), Internal-States+RC, the ICLR-2023 Semantic Entropy anchor, and TSV's TruthfulQA row. Those are badged unverified wherever they appear.
