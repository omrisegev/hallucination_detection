Key Takeaways
  1. Math (GSM8K): Nadler 75.92% (1-pass) matches SE on Mistral-7B (75.85%, K=10) at 10× lower compute. Beats LapEigvals unsupervised (72.0%) without needing attention maps.
  2. Math (MATH-500): 96.69% — almost certainly the first published AUROC for Qwen2.5-Math-7B on MATH-500 in this framing. EDIS 80.4% uses a weaker 1.5B model pooled over 4 datasets. Our own 1.5B variant (87.97%) already beats EDIS by +7.6 pp.
  3. Science (GPQA): Published VC/SC numbers (74.6–82.1%) use models 4–10× stronger (reasoning-class). Our Qwen-72B (67.47%) is competitive for general-purpose models. Awaiting same-model computed baselines.
  4. RAG (L-CiteEval): Novel task — no published AUROC competitor. Best result 88.15% (Llama-8B / HotpotQA), beats LOS-Net 72.9% by +15.3 pp on a related task. 13/16 cells ≥ 70%, median 72.8%. SelfCheckGPT (pending) will be the first same-task baseline.
